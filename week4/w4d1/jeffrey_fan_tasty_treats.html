Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This delicious treat is a staple for all parties. As you can see chocolate chip cookies are great and tasty treats.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 12 ounce semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt

Baking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Melt butter in microwave
  3. Whisk sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla extract in large bowl
  4. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in another bowl
  5. Stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients with wooden spoon
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Roll dough into balls and put on baking sheet
  8. Bake until golden, 12-16 min, depending on how you like your cookies
